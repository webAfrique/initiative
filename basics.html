Basics

Grab attention right from the start; introduce the purpose of your campaign and entice people to learn more. The basic information shared here will be the main focus of your campaign page.

Campaign title

Your title is more than just words- it’s the gateway to your cause. Choose wisely, for it’s the first glimpse into your mission. Make it resonate, make it memorable, and make it matter, Let your title be the spark that ignites curiosity and rallies support.

Campaign tag line

Your campaign tagline holds the power to convey your mission in a few impactful words. Make it a magnet for attention, a beacon of purpose, and a catalyst for action. Craft a tagline that echoes your cause and resonates with hearts. Ignite curiosity, inspire change, and begin your campaign's journey with a compelling tagline that speaks volumes.

Campaign card image

Your campaign card image is the face of your cause, capturing attention in an instant. Let it tell a story, evoke emotion, and draw people in. It's a window into your mission's soul. Unleash the power of visuals and start your campaign with an image that resonates.

Category

Selecting the right category for your campaign is like choosing the genre for your story. It's how you convey your mission to the world. Make your choice with intention, as it aligns your cause with a community that cares. Your category is the compass that guides your campaign's journey, leading you towards the change you're set to create.

Tags

Tags are like magic words for your campaign. They help people find and understand what you're all about. Pick tags that fit your cause, so your campaign gets noticed by the right folks. With each tag, you're creating connections and inviting others to join your mission. Let your tags work their charm and make your campaign stand out.

Campaign Duration

This is the duration your campaign will run for. Ensure that the chosen duration aligns with your goals and objectives.
